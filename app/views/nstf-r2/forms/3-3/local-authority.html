3.3 Local need and support

Local authority support

Do you have the local authority's support and endorsement for your proposal?

Upload the letter now if you have it.

If you do not have it at the moment, we might contact you by email when we assess your application.

The letter should be written by a rough sleeping lead and must not be written by a local councillor.

Your application will not be considered if you cannot provide the letter of endorsement.

It should be a single file no bigger than 5MB in an accepted format (jpg, jpeg, png, pdf, txt, doc, docx, odt, rtf, csv, xls, xlsx, ods.)
