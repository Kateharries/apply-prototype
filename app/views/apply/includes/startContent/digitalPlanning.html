How to complete your application

What we’ll ask you for

We have outlined the information needed to complete the application form in our prospectus. If you have not received the prospectus, contact digitalplanningteam@communities.gov.uk.

You can preview the full list of application questions.

Moving through the form

The form is split into sections based on how it will be assessed.

You do not have to complete it in order, or all at once.

When you have written something in a field in every section, the section will be marked as complete. You can still change your responses in sections that are marked as complete.

Saving progress and making changes

Your work will not save automatically. To make sure you do not lose anything, select Save and continue regularly, and always before you go back or close your internet browser window.

If you need to edit or revisit any sections, use the browser back button.

You can edit your responses as many times as you like before you submit.

Submitting your application

When you have completed all sections, you’ll be able to submit your application.

Once you have submitted your application, you’ll not be able to edit, add or change any section.

Get help

If you have questions about the form or the fund, contact us

Do not send any applications or attachments through the support desk. We'll only accept applications through this service.

How we’ll use your information

Read how we’ll use the information from your application in our privacy notice
