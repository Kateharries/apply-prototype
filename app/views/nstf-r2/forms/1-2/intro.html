1.2 Organisation type

In this section, we'll ask about:

  • organisation type
  • charity registration number (if applicable)
  • company registration number (if applicable)
  • names of trustees or committee members (if applicable)
  • organisation operating time (if applicable)

We might ask you to upload:

  • your organisation’s annual accounts from the last 3 years (if applicable)
  • your organisation’s annual accounts that are available (if applicable)
  • a current bank statement from your organisation, less than 2 years old (if applicable)
  • names of trustees or committee members (if applicable)
  • your organisation’s annual budget (if applicable)
